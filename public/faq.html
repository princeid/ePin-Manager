This is a collection of the frequently asked questions about the new MTN ePin Manager Application.

1. Who can use the MTN ePin Manager?

The MTN epin Manager was built for MTN licensed recharge card dealers to enable them purchase recharge cards directly from MTN and sell to distributors.

2. What should I do if my voucher is invalid even after making payment?

This is highly unlikely to occur. However, we advice you to please give us a call on our customer care line in such situation.

3. What should I do if someone gains access to my account?

Our system is highly secured. Hence, we highly recommend that you use a strongly typed password when registering and don't share your password with anyone. However, if someone managed to gain access to your account, please kindly send us a mail or call us as soon as possible.

In case you haven't found the answer to your question please feel free to contact us, our customer support will be happy to help you.
